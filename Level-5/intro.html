Arrays!

Arrays are like magical bags that can hold multiple items at once.

They allow us to store a collection of items, like a bag of apples or a bunch of bananas, in a single variable.

Have you ever needed to keep track of many things at once? Let's explore the power of arrays:

Imagine you have a bag that can hold different fruits like apples, bananas, and oranges!

Here's a simple example using arrays:

        
            # Creating an array of fruits
            fruits = ["apples", "bananas", "oranges"]

            # Accessing elements in the array
            print("My favorite fruit is " + fruits[0])

Array Operations

Arrays support various operations like adding elements, removing elements, and accessing specific elements by their index.
